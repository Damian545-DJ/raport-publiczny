# Timeline (Public Version)

> Dates and descriptions are based on our documentation and evidence report. This public version is anonymized.

## 2023 – early 2024
- Growing employment and payroll issues, disputes over deductions and guaranteed hours (phase C).
- The worker was left without income or work for several months.
- The agency charged a "debt" for company housing.
- A meeting at the Intrixo agency office, during which pressure and intimidation were applied to force the signing of documents disadvantageous to the worker (audio recordings).

## 12.03.2024 – Court hearing
- The hearing took place.
- During the case, an advance payment of EUR 5,000 was to be obtained.
- The court gave both parties time to provide accurate calculations.

## 04.2024
- A meeting to discuss the possibility of another case concerning outstanding settlements. No clear information on the completion of the previous case.
- The attorney did not provide the *proces-verbaal* document.

## 24.05.2024
- Information about the ruling/decision was provided by the accountant; at that time, there was no contact with the attorney.
- The worker repaid a debt charged by the agency (contrary to Dutch law).

## 06.2024 – Hiring a new attorney (MV Juridisch)
- First meeting at the end of June.
- Declared duration of the case: about 3 months.

## 09.2024 – 2025 and beyond
- Lack of communication and updates.
- Information that documents could not be obtained from the previous lawyer.
- Later: claim that a settlement had been reached and "nothing could be done", without showing any documentation.
- Ultimately: no refund of paid money and the appearance of unclear invoices for "no work done" and additional payments (suspected fraud).

## 2025
- Complaint filed against the previous lawyer to the *Orde van Advocaten*, followed by proceedings before the Disciplinary Council and an appeal.
- The authors personally obtained the court document (*proces-verbaal*) and again sent letters to relevant entities requesting the case documentation (from the first attorney) and fulfillment of the settlement terms (by Intrixo) – no response.
