## 3. Intrixo acquisition — direct ACM source

On 18 September 2025, the Netherlands Authority for Consumers and Markets (ACM) published a concentration notification stating that Home of People Acquisitie 3 B.V. intended to acquire all issued shares in Intrixo B.V. ACM also stated that Intrixo B.V., together with its participation in Masterteam B.V., formed the Intrixo Group.

ACM later stated that it permitted the acquisition by decision of 29 September 2025 because sufficient competition would remain after the transaction. The final notice was published on 1 October 2025.

Official sources:

- ACM concentration notification: https://www.acm.nl/nl/publicaties/home-people-wil-intrixo-overnemen-concentratiemelding
- ACM final notice: https://www.acm.nl/nl/publicaties/home-people-mag-intrixo-overnemen-eindmededeling

ACM's approval concerns merger control and competition. It is not an assessment of individual payroll, employment or accommodation practices.
