PUBLIC BRIEFING

For institutions — verification briefing

This is a short, anonymized public briefing prepared for institutions and bodies assessing the documentation.

Last updated: 2026-07-02 · Public version: briefing / anonymized

Purpose of the document

The purpose of this page is to indicate quickly which documents and discrepancies may require independent review: employment documents, payroll records, deductions, working-hour guarantees and the practical effectiveness of legal protection.

The public material is not presented as a final judgment. It is a structured basis for further independent assessment.

What should be checked

  • the completeness and consistency of payroll documents, payslips, bank transfers and settlement records;
  • the settlement of the 32-hour working-time guarantee and work scheduling;
  • the correctness of deductions, balances and the gross-to-net calculation;
  • whether calculations are complete, clear and verifiable;
  • whether court-stage arrangements were practically executed;
  • whether institutions and legal representatives addressed the key documents.

Access to materials

The public layer is anonymized. Fuller materials may be shared only through a justified and secure channel, with respect for data protection.

Core question

Were rights and procedures only formally available, or were they actually executed, verified and effectively protected in practice?
