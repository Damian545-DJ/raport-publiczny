Purpose of this page

The purpose is to show quickly what material exists in the repository and where to find fuller, structured descriptions.

Core question: whether formal rights and available procedures were effective in practice.

Suggested verification points

  • guaranteed working hours and work scheduling;
  • payslips, payroll records, bank transfers and deductions;
  • balances and settlement calculations;
  • court-related documents and follow-up actions;
  • institutional correspondence and practical access to protection;
  • whether the public file shows a wider issue of effective protection for migrant workers.

The public material is not presented as a final judgment. It is a structured basis for further independent review.

New materials

Recent additions include the Your Europe Advice response, materials concerning legal assistance and further correspondence with institutions.

These materials strengthen the need to check whether protection was effective in practice, not only formally available.
