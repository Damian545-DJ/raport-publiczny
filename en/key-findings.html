Two different scopes that must not be merged

Obligation recorded in the proces-verbaal

Within two weeks Intrixo had to provide the worker's representative with a calculation of gross wages due since 6 May 2022.

Data needed to verify the final balance

Independently testing a no-further-claim conclusion additionally requires a gross-to-net reconciliation, deductions, bank payments, separate treatment of the EUR 5,000 advance and the final balance.

Important: the proces-verbaal does not expressly state that all of those additional elements had to appear in one document.

1. The proces-verbaal imposed a specific wage-calculation obligation on Intrixo

Date: 12 March 2024documented

The proces-verbaal states that Intrixo had to provide the worker's representative, within two weeks, with a calculation of gross wages due since 6 May 2022.

Public source: anonymized proces-verbaal excerpt

2. The two-week period ended on 26 March 2024, but the public material contains no proof of full performance by that date

Date: 26 March 2024requires verification

The date follows from the two-week period beginning on 12 March 2024. The absence of public proof does not by itself establish final non-performance; the complete file and correspondence must be compared.

Public source: the obligation and its two-week deadline

3. The Phase 4 / Phase C agreement provided for 32 working hours per week

Start date: 21 November 2022documented

Public source: Phase 4 / Phase C agreement excerpt
