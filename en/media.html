For media and institutions

This page provides a careful, public-facing overview for editorial teams, NGOs and institutions assessing the case documentation.

Publicly available

  • anonymized summaries and timeline,
  • public evidence index references,
  • source links to Markdown documentation.

May be shared after justified contact

  • broader document sets within data-protection limits,
  • selected audio/video for verified review access,
  • supporting technical extracts for fact-checking.

Limits from anonymization and data protection

  • no publication of sensitive personal data,
  • restricted handling of full-source files,
  • scope of sharing depends on purpose and secure processing safeguards.

Legal caution

The report presents allegations/claims together with documentation. It is not a final legal determination and not legal advice.
