For media and institutions (EN)

This page summarizes public material and safe contact rules for editorial/institutional review.

What is public

  • anonymized event descriptions,
  • timeline and evidence index,
  • institutional correspondence map.

What can be shared after justified contact

  • expanded source documentation,
  • audio/video recordings for verified review access,
  • supporting technical tables for fact-checking.

Legal caution

Published content describes allegations/claims based on documentation. It is not a final judicial finding and not legal advice.

Contact

  • Repository: Damian545-DJ/raport-publiczny
  • First contact: GitHub Issue/Discussion with outlet/institution identity and verification purpose.
  • After initial verification: secure transfer channel agreed case-by-case.
