Timeline — key dates and calculation changes

This chronology uses the public anonymized material. Where a full document is not public, the description says so and does not replace independent review of the complete file.

Last modified: 2026-07-14 · Sources last verified: 2026-07-11

  1. 6 May 2022

    Start of the period covered by the required calculation

    The process-verbaal of 12 March 2024 states that the calculation had to cover gross wages due from 6 May 2022.

    Public source: process-verbaal excerpt

  2. 21 November 2022

    Start of the NBBU Fase 4 agreement for 32 hours per week

    This was an agreement under NBBU Fase 4, equivalent to ABU Phase C: indefinite-term, 32 hours per week and not an on-call contract.

    Public source: Fase 4 agreement excerpt

  3. 12 March 2024

    Process-verbaal and a specific obligation imposed on Intrixo

    Within two weeks Intrixo had to provide the worker's representative with a calculation of gross wages due from 6 May 2022. This was an obligation of Intrixo, not a general task for both parties.

    Public source: anonymized excerpt

  4. 18 March 2024

    EUR 5,000 advance payment

    The banking package contains confirmation of a EUR 5,000 transfer. Payment of the advance was a separate element and did not replace the obligation to provide the full gross-wage calculation.

    The complete bank confirmation remains non-public.

  5. 26 March 2024

    Expiry of the two-week deadline

    The deadline under the process-verbaal expired two weeks after 12 March 2024. The public material contains no confirmation that a complete gross-wage calculation from 6 May 2022 had been provided by that date; this requires review of the full file.

  6. 1 April 2026

    First calculation: approximately EUR 3,503.05 gross

    The third representative presented a first calculation while also stating that not all payslips were available.

    Source: full public timeline

  7. 7 April 2026

    Second calculation: approximately EUR 4,155.85 gross

    After receiving “almost all” missing payslips, the result increased. A no-further-claim position was nevertheless stated while the documentation was still incomplete.

  8. 8 April 2026

    Written identification of calculation errors

    Specific numerical discrepancies, disputed weeks and partial alternative calculations were submitted. An organized banking-evidence package followed.

  9. 7 May 2026

    Corrected calculation: EUR 7,741.03 gross

    After the errors and additional materials were submitted, the result increased from approximately EUR 4,155.85 to EUR 7,741.03, an increase of about EUR 3,585.18 gross. No single complete table covering the entire period from 6 May 2022, all transfers, deductions, the advance and the final balance was produced.

    Source: detailed description of the successive calculations

  10. 29 June 2026

    Raad van Discipline decision, reference 25-714/DH/DH

    According to the decision held by the authors, the objection was upheld, complaint parts a) and d) were upheld and a reprimand was imposed. The full decision is not published on this site.

    Public source: anonymized operative-part summary

  11. July 2026

    Safe organization and update of the public material

    Following the disciplinary decision, the anonymized report, key findings, five-evidence page and PL/EN/NL versions were updated. Personal data, full payslips, bank statements and unredacted case files were not published.
