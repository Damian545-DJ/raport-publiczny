What this section is

A supplementary context layer to the main Intrixo report. It brings together:

  • publicly available information about Sezer Duygulu and companies publicly linked to him or to the broader Home of People ecosystem,
  • public court, registry, media and certification materials concerning parts of that ecosystem,
  • documented inconsistencies referenced in the author’s own Intrixo-related case, presented as context and not as a final legal determination.

What this section is not

  • It is not a court judgment.
  • It does not by itself establish personal civil, criminal or administrative liability.
  • It does not ask the reader to rely on headlines alone.
  • It should be read together with the source archive and verified against primary materials.

Why a separate section exists

The main Intrixo report focuses on one employment dispute. The Sezer Duygulu section exists because some public corporate, legal and contextual materials may help readers understand the wider environment in which that dispute sits.

What matters most for a first read

Start with the short language page, then move to the long Markdown source only if you need the full archive, the detailed case chain, or the full source list.
