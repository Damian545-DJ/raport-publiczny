# Public context of entities and the Home of People group

This file is a neutral public description of organizational context. It is informational and supplementary to the main report concerning the Intrixo case.

The material is not a judgment, accusation or assignment of responsibility to any person or entity. Each item should be checked against primary sources, registers, official publications and documents.

## Purpose

The purpose of this section is to show that the Intrixo case exists within a broader environment of entities operating in temporary work, recruitment, accommodation and payroll settlement for migrant workers.

The mere existence of organizational, marketing or historical links does not establish responsibility for specific events. It is contextual only.

## Scope

Public materials mention, as context, entities and brands such as Home of People, Uitzendbureau Solutions, T&S Flexwerk, NWH Jobs, Efficient at Work, Intrixo / Voorneputten and Masterteam.

This context may be relevant for institutions, legal representatives, organizations and media because the case concerns a model in which work, housing, payroll settlement and economic dependence of the worker may overlap.

## Public-version safety rules

The public version should not publish private data, exact accommodation details, room numbers, raw recordings, faces, voices, document numbers, bank-account numbers or other identifying elements.
