# Sezer Duygulu and Home of People – public context of entities and documented inconsistencies

This section supplements the main report with public organizational context. It is not an accusation, judgment or automatic assignment of responsibility to any individual.

The name of a person publicly connected with business activity is used only to identify public materials. Private-worker data and exact accommodation and workplace locations have been removed.

---

## 1. Purpose and methodology

- compile publicly available information about the group and brands,
- separate confirmed sources from assessments and questions requiring further review,
- provide context for the individual Intrixo case,
- use cautious language and the principle that a link does not establish responsibility.

---

## 2. Entities appearing in the public context

- Home of People
- Uitzendbureau Solutions
- Intrixo / Voorneputten
- T&S Flexwerk
- NWH Jobs
- Efficient at Work
- Masterteam

---

## 3. Nature of the links

Public industry and corporate materials describe Home of People as a group combining or acquiring brands active in temporary work, recruitment and services for migrant workers.

A capital, organizational, marketing or historical link does not determine responsibility for another entity's conduct. It may nevertheless help explain the operating model and supervisory structure.

---

## 4. Public consolidation timeline

- 2022 – announced rebranding of Solutions Group as Home of People,
- 2023 – public information concerning NWH Jobs joining the group,
- 2024 – public information concerning the acquisition of Efficient at Work,
- 2025 – public materials concerning the acquisition of Intrixo and Masterteam,
- the date and scope of each transaction should always be checked in primary sources.

## 6. Meaning of the judgments

The identifiers are included to locate public sources. The proceedings do not all have the same nature, not all were brought directly against every named entity, and their outcomes cannot automatically be transferred to another case.

The strongest public line of judgments concerns Uitzendbureau Solutions, termination connected with illness and the subsequent wage settlement. A separate public employment case concerns Intrixo.

---

## 7. Individual Intrixo case

- the main report describes a Phase C contract and 32-hour guarantee,
- the documentation contains disputed hour, wage and deduction settlements,
- the proces-verbaal required Intrixo to calculate gross wages from 6 May 2022,
- the group-context section does not replace analysis of the individual worker's documents.

---

## 8. Work–accommodation model

- combining employment, transport and housing can increase a worker's economic dependence,
- deductions and job loss may directly affect housing security,
- this is a systemic risk requiring transparent procedures,
- the model itself does not prove a violation in an individual case.

---

## 9. Documented inconsistencies

- the authors identify differences between correspondence, planning, payroll documents and later explanations,
- each difference must be assessed against the full source and chronology,
- the repository distinguishes a document, the author's account and an inference requiring verification.

---

## 10. Research limitations

- public registers may be incomplete or require payment,
- ownership structures and individual roles may change over time,
- failure to find a published judgment does not prove that no other proceedings exist,
- secondary information should be confirmed through primary documents.

---

## 11. Anonymization rules

- exact accommodation addresses and workplaces are not published,
- private-worker initials are not published,
- document and account numbers, signatures, faces and raw recordings are not published,
- company names and judgment identifiers remain only where needed to identify a public source.

---

## 12. Conclusion

The public material supports further careful review of worker-risk management, documentation, deductions and accommodation within the described ecosystem.

It does not constitute a final finding of personal guilt by Sezer Duygulu or automatic liability of Home of People or every brand for another entity's conduct.
